# 🛡️ FoE Treasury Monitor v3.8.1 - Universal Edition

**Automated Guild Treasury management at your fingertips.**
Take full control of your guild's resources with an automated, high-precision monitoring tool that sends real-time, color-coded reports directly to your **Discord**.

## 🚀 Key Features
*   **🌐 Full Era Coverage:** Supports all eras from **Iron Age** to **Space Age: Space Hub**.
*   **📊 Smart Monitoring:** Track stock levels with 100% accuracy.
*   **🚨 Dual Alert System:**
    *   **🔸Orange Alert:** Warning when stock falls below your limit.
    *   **🚨Red Critical Alert:** Triggers when stock levels hit a critical percentage.
*   **🛡️ Safety First:** A **passive script** that only reads exported CSV data. Zero risk of bans.
*   **🧹 Built-in Maintenance:** Automatically archives reports and cleans up old files.

## 🛠️ How to Use
1.  **Configuration:** Open the `config.ini` file and paste your **Discord webhook URL**.
2.  **Set Your Limits:** Customize your minimum stock requirements for specific goods or entire eras.
3.  **Data Export:** Export your treasury data as a **CSV** file using the FoE Helper extension.
4.  **Launch:** Run the monitor to receive your professional guild report instantly.
